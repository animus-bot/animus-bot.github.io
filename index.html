Features

Fun

Explore a world of endless amusement with Animus' fun commands! Engage your server members in trivia quizzes, funny games, and entertaining activities that are sure to keep everyone entertained. Discover a whole new level of fun with Animus by your side!

Moderation

Administer your server efficiently with Animus' comprehensive moderation commands. Manage your community, maintain order, and ensure compliance with your server rules. Experience the power to kick, ban, warn, mute, and manage roles with ease. With Animus, server management has never been more streamlined!

Management

Unlock the full potential of your server with Animus' management commands. Leverage the tools to enhance user engagement, promote active participation, and optimize server functionality. From automating server tasks to scheduling events, managing roles and permissions, and organizing channel structure, Animus empowers you to create a thriving community environment tailored to your server's unique needs.

Utility

Maximize your server's functionality with Animus' utility commands. From quickly looking up information to managing server settings and members, Animus streamlines various tasks to make server administration and user experience seamless and efficient.
